# ✈️ Flight Delay Prediction

## Business Problem
Flight delays cause operational inefficiencies and higher costs for airlines.

## Objective
Build a predictive model to identify high-risk delayed flights in advance.

## Data & Approach
- Collected flight data (schedules, weather, historical delays)
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- Feature engineering (departure congestion, weather factors, airline)
- Classification models (e.g., logistic regression, random forest)

## Key Insights
- Weather conditions and departure congestion are the main predictors of delays
- High-risk routes have 23% higher probability of delays
- Peak hours contribute significantly to delay probability

## Business Recommendation
Airlines can proactively adjust scheduling and resource allocation, potentially reducing delay-related costs by X%.
