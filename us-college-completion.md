# 🎓 US College Completion Dashboard & Analytics

## 📌 Business Problem
Higher education stakeholders — including policymakers, administrators, and school systems — need actionable insights into **college completion trends** across states, regions, and demographics. Without digestible dashboards, decision makers struggle to understand where improvements or interventions are most needed.

---

## 🎯 Objective
Develop an **interactive analytical dashboard** that visualizes US college completion rates over time and across different demographic groups, so decision makers can quickly derive actionable insights for planning and policy.

---

## 📍 Data & Analytical Approach

### 📊 Data
- US college enrollment and completion data from national datasets  
- Demographic variables: gender, ethnicity, state, region  
- Time variables: year, cohort

---

### 🛠 Methodology
1. **Data Cleaning & Wrangling**
   - Handled missing values and inconsistent formatting  
   - Harmonized demographic categories and time periods

2. **Metric Definition**
   - Defined key KPIs such as:
     - College enrollment rate  
     - Completion (graduation) rate  
     - Year-over-year progress

3. **Exploratory Analysis**
   - Analyzed trends by state and over time  
   - Breakdown of completion rates by demographic groups  
   - Identified persistent gaps between groups

4. **Dashboard Development**
   - Built interactive visualizations using Tableau  
   - Dashboard filters for state, region, and demographic slicers

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- **Regional Disparities:** Some regions consistently outperform others in graduation rates — highlighting areas that may need targeted policy intervention.  
- **Demographic Gaps:** Completion rates vary significantly by gender and ethnicity, signaling equity challenges in access and support.  
- **Time Trends:** Overall national completion has improved year‑by‑year, but certain groups lag behind the aggregate trend.

---

## 💡 Business & Policy Recommendations

✔ **Targeted Support:** Allocate resources and student support services to regions and demographic groups with lower completion rates.  
✔ **Equity Strategies:** Develop targeted interventions addressing barriers for underrepresented groups to improve equity outcomes.  
✔ **Performance Monitoring:** Use dashboard views to track progress over time and evaluate program effectiveness.
